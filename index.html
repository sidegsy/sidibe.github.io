I received my Matser (M.Sc.) degree in Network and Mobile computing from International Francophone Institute IFI (Hanoi, Vietnam) in co-graduation with Université Claude Bernard de Lyon 1 (France) in 2016. I did my Master internship in CIDRE team, CentraleSupélec Rennes, IRISA laboratory. I am currently doing a PhD under the direction of Pr. Michel Misson and Dr. Marie-Françoise Servajean on coupling directional antennas in the lower layers of a wireless sensor network.
